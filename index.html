About Us

Independence through innovation

i-Fi glasses provide a holistic solution for visually impaired individuals, integrating advanced technology to enhance mobility and independence. Equipped with object recognition, navigation assistance, and staircase detection, the system ensures a safe and seamless experience in daily life. By processing real-time data, i-Fi glasses offer instant audio alerts and haptic feedback, helping users navigate obstacles with confidence. The intelligent AI adapts to various environments, ensuring reliable performance indoors and outdoors. Our mission is to empower the blind community by making navigation more accessible and effortless. Through cutting-edge innovation, i-Fi glasses break barriers, promote self-reliance, and improve the quality of life for visually impaired individuals worldwide.

OUR SERVICES

Stair Detection

i-Fi glasses enhance safety by accurately detecting staircases and elevation changes. The system differentiates steps from flat surfaces, providing instant vibration and audio alerts. Advanced technology measures height, depth, and movement shifts, ensuring precise recognition. Pre-mapped locations further assist navigation in public spaces, reducing missteps and promoting independent mobility.

Object & Person Detection

i-Fi glasses use advanced AI to detect objects and individuals in real time, ensuring accurate obstacle recognition. Instant audio alerts and haptic feedback enhance safety, adapting seamlessly to indoor and outdoor environments. By providing real-time awareness, i-Fi glasses reduce risks and empower users with independent, smart navigation.

SafeTrack

i-Fi glasses use advanced technology to detect obstacles, elevation changes, and individuals in real time. Audio alerts and haptic feedback ensure safe, independent navigation indoors and outdoors. With intelligent recognition and proactive warnings, i-Fi enhances mobility, making navigation safer and more effortless.

GPS Tracking

With intelligent recognition and proactive warnings, i-Fi enhances mobility, making navigation safer and more effortless.

Customized Smart Glasses

i-Fi glasses offer a personalized fit, adapting to age, face shape, and size preferences for maximum comfort. With adjustable frames and ergonomic design, they ensure easy use for all, blending technology and accessibility seamlessly.

Multi-Language Support

i-Fi glasses automatically detect and translate languages, providing real-time audio feedback in the user’s preferred language. This ensures seamless communication, enhancing accessibility and independence for visually impaired users.
